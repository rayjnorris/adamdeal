AUTO

Juxtaposed to stagnation and disintegration, the automobile reflects our collective drive towards the future, symbolic of an irresistible urge to move faster than nature intended. To encounter these vestiges of speed as stationary objects of decay, is to witness the completion of a circle composed of human ambition and nature's will.
