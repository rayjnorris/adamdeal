REFLECT

Reflections embody the dream-like nature of our post-modern world. Glass, light, shapes and color intermingle, converging two scenes. One adds to the other, subtracting from visual coherence. As optical familiarity decreases, the ability to see directly increases. What is gained, when discernment is lost?
